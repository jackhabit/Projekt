The Strawhat Riots

In 1922, New York City saw one of the most crazy events in history known as the Straw Hat Riot. Although there was no weird law against it, back then wearing a straw hat after September 15th was considered to be a fashion crime. Those spotted wearing them would usually be ridiculed in public or have their hats knocked off their heads. This weird practice started when gangs started forcibly removing straw hats from people’s heads, as wearing them after mid-September was considered a breach of fashion decorum. People started taking it so seriously that it even sparked violent riots that lasted for days. While no deaths were reported, many people were injured and police had to eventually intervene to control the mayhem. All this over some straw hats! Prohibition in New York and the Straw Riots are events that happened at the same time.
